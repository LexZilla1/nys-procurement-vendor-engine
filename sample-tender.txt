STATE OF NEW YORK - INVITATION FOR BIDS (IFB)
Solicitation No. RFP-2026-0042  -  Facilities Maintenance Services
Issuing Agency: NYS Office of General Services
Bids are due no later than 2:00 PM on August 15, 2026.
The estimated contract value for this procurement is $250,000.
All bidders must be registered in the NYS Vendor File prior to award.SECTION 3 - MANDATORY BIDDER REQUIREMENTS
The contractor shall submit an Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) policy statement.
Bidders are required to submit an MWBE utilization plan; the goal for this contract is 30%.
The bidder must provide a certificate of general liability insurance of at least $2,000,000.
A bid bond equal to five percent (5%) of the bid amount is required to accompany each bid.
Each bidder shall complete the Vendor Responsibility Questionnaire (VendRep).
Each bidder must execute a non-collusive bidding certification under State Finance Law 139-d.
Bidders must certify compliance with the Iran Divestment Act under State Finance Law 165-a.
Each bidder must file the Tax Law 5-a sales tax certification for contracts over $100,000.SECTION 4 - SUPPLEMENTAL PROVISIONS
The State has an SDVOB participation goal of 6% for this procurement.
Contractors must maintain workers' compensation coverage for the contract term.
The selected vendor will be required to hold all certifications current through the contract term.
Bidders must certify a sexual harassment prevention policy under State Finance Law 139-l.
Bidders must certify a gender-based violence and the workplace policy under State Finance Law 139-m.
Contractors on public work must hold a NYSDOL Certificate of Registration under Labor Law 220-i.
Contractors agree not to participate in an international boycott under State Finance Law 139-h.
Questions regarding this solicitation must be submitted prior to the deadline.
