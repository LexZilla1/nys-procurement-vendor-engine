## OSC GUIDANCE — ENTITY EXCLUSIONS (verbatim, secondary source — GFO XII.5.I)

Separate from the § 179-p statutory payment-type list, OSC's Guide to Financial Operations
XII.5.I enumerates entities NOT entitled to prompt-payment interest. Captured verbatim
2026-07-01 from https://www.osc.ny.gov/state-agencies/gfo/chapter-xii/xii5i-prompt-payment-interest :

Per SFL § 179-p, the following payment types or entities are not entitled to prompt payment interest:
- local governments, including but not limited to, counties, cities, towns, villages, school
  districts, special districts and their related entities
- public authorities and public benefit corporations
- state employees working in their public employment capacity
- third party payment agreement contractors, including but not limited to, the fiscal agent or
  intermediary under Social Services Law § 367-b
